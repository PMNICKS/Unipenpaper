Payment Procedure

Unienpaper.com makes all the payments twice per month - every 1st-5th and 16th-20th of every month. The funds withdrawal takes place once per payout, on the 1st and 16th of the month. The detailed procedure of the funds withdrawal as well as funds transfer is available on writer’s personal profile (Payment Statistics page > Payment process).

In order to be included into the payment period, writer’s account must have payment details submitted in advance, with writer’s balance meeting the minimum amount ($100) on the evening before the withdrawal day.

Payment option details should be provided at least 4 days prior to the beginning of the payment period (till the 12th and 27th of each month correspondingly). Payment details must belong to the writer account owner.

If the writer chooses to stop working for unienpaper.com, the outstanding balance will be released to the writer.

After the termination of accounts, writers are not allowed to change the payment details that were submitted earlier.



Payment options we provide:

WebMoney

Payoneer

Two payment options are supported by this payment system:

  • Global Bank Transfer - receive payments directly to your bank account, in your local currency. This option supports payments to more than 200 countries, in over 50 currencies, starting at only $2.99 per transfer. Easy one-time setup of bank details and no maintenance fees.
  • Payoneer Prepaid MasterCard - 0% commission. Get paid to your very own Prepaid Debit MasterCard®. No bank account required and funds are available within 2 hours after the payment was released.

WebMoney

  • 10% commission. If you choose this payment option, you have to provide us a valid email address that you get assigned at registration. It takes 1-2 hours for the payment to get processed.

In order to select one of them, you need to log into your unienpaper account and open “Payment statistics” section and submit the preferred payment method.
