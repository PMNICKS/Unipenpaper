How to get started?

01

Register at unipenpaper.com

Fill in the registration form and provide us with the valid personal and contact information, so that we could contact you in case we have any urgent questions regarding your writing projects.

02

Read a tutorial and take short quizzes on it

Look through our short tutorial which consists of 4 parts and is aimed to help you get acquainted with the field of academic writing. Each part ends with a short quiz of 3-5 questions. In order to move forward, you need to pass all 4 quizzes. Please be attentive as you have to know the information provided for a successful cooperation with our team.

03

Upload your essay, diploma and CV

Write a sample essay on the given topic, upload a photo of your higher education certificate as well as your CV. Upon your desire, you may also add some samples of your previous works to show us your level of academic writing.

Once you complete the last step, your application will be sent for review. We will get back to you with a decision on whether you can join our Team. The review may take from 2 days to 2 weeks.

How it works?

01

Select the writing project

When a customer orders an academic writing assignment, we need to find the writer to complete it. It could be you! We receive various writing projects daily and you will definitely find one that matches your area of expertise. If you have any questions about the order, you can always contact either the client or the Writer Support Team (available 24/7).

02

Upload the work

As soon as the work is finished, you need to upload it into your personal account as well as mark it as “Final”. The client reviews the work and, if they are satisfied with the quality, the money for the order gets credited to your account. In case the work requires revision, you will be given an additional time to amend it. Please note that both clients and our representatives can send orders for amendments that you are expected to make.

03

Get paid for your work

We offer you a competitive salary and good profit. With us you can get up to $20 per page and earn part-time or even full-time income. Please note that you get paid twice per month; however, you need to choose the payment option first. To get more detailed information, please visit our Payments page.
